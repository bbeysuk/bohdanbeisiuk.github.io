DesignRush 2019–2022

Scaling a B2B Directory from Zero to 14k Agencies

The Challenge

A 4-person startup wanted to become the go-to B2B directory for brands to find agencies. They had zero agencies in the directory and no team to source them.

The Result

Built outreach team from scratch (0→30 people), designed and executed large-scale prospecting campaigns, and grew directory from 0→14,000 agencies in 3 years. Company scaled to 100+ employees.
